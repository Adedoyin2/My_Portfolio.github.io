about me

Hello! I’m Ogunwusi Adedoyin Noah, the founder of NOABYTE TECH, a digital tech company with a mission to simplify technology and empower individuals and businesses through innovative solutions. I’m a passionate Web Developer, Graphics Designer, Video Editor, UI/UX Designer, Social Media Manager and A Bloger with years of hands-on experience in the tech space. I earned my Higher National Diploma (HND) in Computer Science from the Federal School of Statistics, Ibadan, a reflection of my commitment to excellence from the very start. At the heart of my work is a genuine love for creativity and problem-solving. Whether I’m building functional websites, designing compelling graphics, or editing high-quality videos, I approach every project with purpose, professionalism, and attention to detail. My passion for tech goes beyond service delivery, I’ve trained and mentored others in the field, helping them gain practical skills and confidence to pursue tech careers of their own. Over the years, I’ve received glowing feedback from clients and collaborators, many of whom have rated my work 4 or 5 stars based on satisfaction and impact. My ability to communicate clearly, lead effectively, and work with integrity has helped me stand out — not just as a tech expert, but as a team builder and problem solver. At NOABYTE TECH, we don’t just build products — we create experiences that drive growth and transformation. Whether you're a business looking to go digital or an individual in need of professional tech services, I’m here to help you succeed. Above all, I’m a passionate believer, and my relationship with God is the foundation of everything I do. It has shaped my character, fueled my growth, and guided my impact. Let’s work together, because with the right support, tech doesn’t have to be complicated. You can also find me on Socials @Noah Ogunwusi
